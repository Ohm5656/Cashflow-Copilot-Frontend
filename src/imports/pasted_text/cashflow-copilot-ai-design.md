Design a modern AI financial assistant app for SMEs called “Cashflow Copilot AI”

The product helps SMEs manage cash flow, detect financial risks, predict cash runway, and simulate business decisions.

The design must be:

• Mobile-first (app-like interface)
• Responsive for mobile, tablet, laptop, desktop
• Dark theme with black + glowing pink accent
• Clean, modern, futuristic fintech design
• User-friendly and cute but professional
• Neon glow effects for key metrics
• Smooth rounded cards and soft shadows

Language: Thai

Primary color theme:

Background: deep black / charcoal

Accent: glowing pink / magenta

Secondary accent: soft purple

Text: white / light gray
