# ✦ Cashflow Copilot AI
### AI-Powered CFO Dashboard for SMEs

> Understand your cash. Predict your future. Run your business smarter.

Cashflow Copilot AI is a modern fintech dashboard designed to help small and medium businesses understand their financial health, monitor cash runway, and simulate business scenarios.

The platform acts like an **AI CFO assistant**, helping entrepreneurs visualize their financial situation and make better decisions about revenue, expenses, and survival runway.

## ✨ Features

### 📊 Financial Health Dashboard
- AI Risk Score for business financial health
- Expense anomaly detection
- Product profitability insights
- Dead stock identification

### 💰 Cash Flow Intelligence
- Cash Runway calculation
- Current cash balance from bank statements
- Critical date prediction (when cash runs out)
- Cash reconciliation system

### 🧪 Scenario Simulation
Simulate business changes in real time.

- Adjust **revenue**
- Adjust **expenses**
- See how **cash runway changes**
- Visualize projected **cash flow decline over time**

### 📦 Supply Management
- Manage raw materials and inventory
- Track cost structure
- Analyze impact on profit margins

### 📂 Financial Data Import
Upload business financial data:

- Bank Statements
- Invoices / Bills
- Sales data

Used to power the AI financial analysis.

### 🧭 Interactive User Guide
Built-in onboarding guide that explains:

- Financial dashboard
- Cash management tools
- Simulation system
- Business insights

## 🧠 Why This Exists

Many SMEs fail not because they lack revenue, but because they **run out of cash without realizing it early enough**.

Cashflow Copilot AI helps founders answer critical questions like:

- How long can my business survive?
- What happens if revenue drops?
- What happens if costs increase?
- Which products generate profit vs dead stock?
